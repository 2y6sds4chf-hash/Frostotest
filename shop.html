What the rules allow

The shop follows the campaign buying rules from the Frostgrave Second Edition book: wizards may hire soldiers, buy an apprentice, buy any grimoire for 500gc, buy any scroll for 250gc, and freely buy potions, magic weapons, armour, and magic items at their listed purchase prices.
