Creation order

Start with the school, then let the app keep the rules straight.

Starter mode enforces the opening campaign build: 3 spells from your own school, 1 from each aligned school, 2 from different neutral schools, none from the opposed school, 400gc total, and no more than 4 specialist soldiers.

Starter checklist

  • Choose a wizard name and school.
  • Pick 8 starting spells using school relations.
  • Wizard starts at M6 F+2 S+0 A10 W+4 H14.
  • Apprentice costs 100gc and mirrors the wizard with lower F/W/H.
  • You begin with 400gc and up to 8 soldiers, max 4 specialists.

1. Identity and school

Pick the school first. The spell browser and casting numbers update from that choice.

2. Spellbook

Pick spells in order: own school first, then aligned schools, then your two other schools. Tap a spell row to open its full rule text.

3. Wizard, apprentice, and base details

Wizard stats start at the book defaults. Apprentice stats are derived automatically from the wizard.

4. Warband builder

Choose up to eight soldier slots. The app totals gold, counts specialists, and fills the sheet preview for you.

5. Sheet preview

This is the printable version. Use your browser’s Save as PDF option to keep a completed sheet.
